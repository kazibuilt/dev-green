DRAWER SYSTEMS Trusted, stable and consistent in design. The high-front drawer has been a classic for quite some time when it comes to reaching new heights. And thanks to the elegant design lines, the drawer can be adapted perfectly to the furniture as desired. NP Vionaro combines Dynapro with consummate design in an extremely slim drawer side. A smart solution. Vionaro features the best of Dynapro. A slide for wooden and Vionaro drawers. With Dynapro, the proven concealed slide from GRASS, Vionaro uses the same basis as previous wooden drawers — fully synchronised for optimal running characteristics, high stability and load capacities of up to 70 kg.
