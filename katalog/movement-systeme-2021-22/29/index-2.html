29 Synchronisation for excellent running characteristics. Unlike conventional concealed slide systems, Dynapro features a synchronisation mechanism. Even under high load, the excellent running characteristics of Dynapro remain unchanged. Load capacities of up to 70 kg are no problem for the system. Abrasion-resistant plastic rollers en- sure a smooth, even glide every time. Four dimensions for perfect alignment. Dynapro offers integrated height adjustment, side adjustment, tilt angle adjustment and depth adjust- ment. All adjustments are tool-free, readily accessible and easy to make. In short, it takes just a few tweaks to achieve optimal alignment. Four options for greater operating comfort. The successful Soft-close damping system closes Dynapro drawers gently and quietly. The Tipmatic mechanical opening system allows handle-free fronts that can be opened with a gentle tap. The new Tipmatic Soft-close opening system combines all the advantages of mechanical opening with the comfort of the proven Soft-close damping. In addition, GRASS is rounding off its range with Sensomatic — the elec- tronically assisted opening system.
