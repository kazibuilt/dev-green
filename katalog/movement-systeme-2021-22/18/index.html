18 KINVARO FLAP SYSTEMS The ideal storage space is horizontal. Horizontal storage spaces with lift-up flaps at eye level to make them easily accessible and particularly practical. Directly above the worktop in the kitchen, over the sink or hob. Above the bathroom sink or next to the mirror. Above a desk or TV in the living area. In modern interior design, flap cabinets have firmly established themselves as horizontal design elements. Whereas high wall units with high flap fronts used to be the norm, designers today are increasingly returning to horizontal for- mats. Kinvaro T-Slim is the ideal solution. A powerful trio for greater versatility. Wide fronts are as popular as ever. Their advan- tages are particularly clear when used in wall cabinets. Wide fronts not only eliminate gaps, but when opened they reveal plenty of storage space. There is no question that Kinvaro T-Slim also offers an intelligent solution for wide fronts. An additional flap fitting is added for cabinet widths greater than 1200 mm. This creates a powerful trio that increases the load capacity by 50%. Kinvaro T-Slim flap system.
