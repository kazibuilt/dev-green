27 Vionaro combines functional technology with perfect form. The Vionaro drawer sides are formed from a single piece, maintain- ing their completely smooth surface. With two 13 mm slim drawer sides at a perfect 90° angle, the straight-lined design of the drawer system makes a real statement. The powder-coated surfaces in three colours fit perfectly into any modern living style. The intelligent de- tails mean installation is effortless. Simply connect the drawer sides to the bottom panel — with no need for any additional milled grooves. Four comfort systems for maximum operating comfort. The Soft-close damping system means that Vionaro drawers close gently and quietly. The Tipmatic mechanical opening system allows handle-free fronts that can be opened with a gentle tap. The new Tipmatic Soft-close opening system combines all the advantages of mechanical open- ing with the comfort of the proven Soft-close damping. And the Sen- somatic electromechanical opening system means Vionaro drawers open almost by themselves and guaran- tee maximum operating comfort.
