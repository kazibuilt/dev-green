Web host

We host our website with our order processor Platform SH, Platform.sh GmbH, Marienstr. 90, 50825 Cologne, Germany.

Connection data is processed for the purpose of providing and delivering the website. For the mere purpose of providing and delivering the website, this data shall not be stored after the page request. However, the connection data is stored by our processor for security purposes. The duration of processing for security purposes is variable and ends when those security purposes are no longer required. In addition, our processor anonymises the collected data immediately upon its collection and makes the anonymous data available to us for evaluation in the form of statistics. We use these statistics to troubleshoot and further develop our website.

The legal basis for data processing is the unconditional technical necessity for the provision and delivery of the service you expressly requested with your page request to www.grass.eu in accordance with sec. 96 para. 3 of the German Telecommunications Act (Telekommunikationsgesetz - TKG)

 

Server log files

Connection data is processed for the purpose of monitoring the technical function of our website and to increase the operational security of our web host. In terms of log file rotation, log files are stored until a 100 MB limit is reached and automatically overwritten or deleted after the 100 MB limit is reached. The storage period is approximately 60 days. The legal basis for data processing is the unconditional technical necessity of a server log file as a basic data base for error analysis and for security measures within the framework of the service you expressly requested with your page request to www.grass.eu in accordance with sec. 96 para. 3 of the TKG.

 

Contact form

On our website there is the opportunity to contact us directly by means of a contact form. Upon submitting the contact form, the personal data you have entered will be processed by GRASS GmbH for the purpose of processing your request on the basis of the consent you have given by submitting the form in accordance with art. 6 para. 1 line a of the GDPR. Data processing is carried out until revoked. There is no legal or contractual obligation to provide the personal data. The only consequence of non-provision is that you cannot submit your request and that we cannot process it.

 

Google Analytics;
Insofar as you have given your consent, Google Analytics 4, a web analysis service of Google LLC, is used on this website. The entity responsible for users in the EU/EEA and Switzerland is Google Ireland Limited, Google Building Gordon House, 4 Barrow St, Dublin, D04 E5W5, Ireland ("Google").

Scope of processing
Google Analytics uses cookies that enable analysis of your use of our websites. The information collected by means of cookies about your use of this website is usually transmitted to a Google server in the USA and stored there.

We use the User ID function. The User ID allows us to assign a unique, persistent ID to one or more sessions (and the activities within those sessions) and analyse user behaviour across devices.

With Google Analytics 4, IP addresses are anonymised by default. IP anonymisation means that Google truncates your IP address within European Union member states or in other states party to the Agreement on the European Economic Area. Only in exceptional cases will the full IP address be transferred to a Google server in the USA and shortened there. According to Google, the IP address transmitted by your browser as part of Google Analytics will not be merged with other data from Google.
During your visit to the website, your user behaviour is recorded in the form of "events". Events can be:

  • Page requests
  • First visit to the website
  • Start of session
  • Your "click path", Interaction with the website
  • Scrolls (whenever a user scrolls to the end of the page (90%))
  • Clicks on external links 
  • Internal search requests
  • Interaction with videos
  • File downloads
  • Adverts seen/clicked on
  • Language setting

The following is also recorded:

  • Your approximate location (region)
  • Your IP address (in shortened form)
  • Technical information on your browser and your devices (e.g. language setting, screen resolution)
  • Your Internet provider
  • The referrer URL (the website/advert via which you came to this website )

Purposes of processing
On behalf of the operator of this website, Google will use this information to analyse your use of the website and to create reports regarding website activities. The reports provided by Google Analytics are used to analyse the performance of our website and the success of our marketing campaigns.

Recipients
Recipients of the data are/may be

  • Google Ireland Limited, Gordon House, Barrow Street, Dublin 4, Ireland (as processor in acc. with art. 28 GDPR)
  • Google LLC, 1600 Amphitheatre Parkway Mountain View, CA 94043, USA
  • Alphabet Inc., 1600 Amphitheatre Parkway Mountain View, CA 94043, USA

The possibility that US authorities may access data stored by Google cannot be excluded.

Third-country transfer
If data is processed outside the EU/EEA and the level of data protection is not in accordance with the European standard, we have concluded EU standard contractual clauses with the service provider in order to establish an appropriate level of data protection. Google Ireland's parent company, Google LLC, is based in California, USA. Transfer of data to the USA and access by US authorities to the data stored by Google cannot be ruled out. The USA is currently regarded as a third country from a data protection perspective. You do not have the same rights in that country as you do within the EU/EEA. You may not have any legal remedies against access by authorities.

Storage period
The data sent by us and linked to cookies will be automatically deleted after 14 months. Data that has reached the end of its retention period is automatically deleted once a month.

Legal basis
The legal basis for this data processing is your consent in accordance with art. 6, para. 1, p.1 line a of the GDPR.

Revocation
You may revoke your consent at any time, effective for future processing, by accessing the cookie settings [ADD THE LINK TO THE CONSENT TOOL COOKIE SETTINGS HERE] and changing your selection. The legality of processing carried out based on your consent until the point of revocation remains unaffected by this.

Right of objection

If the processing of your personal data is based on the legitimate interest, you have the right to object to this processing. If there are no compelling legitimate reasons for our processing, the processing of your data will be discontinued on this legal basis. In addition, you have the right to object to the processing of your personal data for the purpose of direct marketing. In the event of an objection, your personal data will no longer be processed for the purpose of direct marketing. The lawfulness of the data processed until the point of objection shall not be affected by the objection.

Right of revocation

You have the right to revoke your consent at any time. In the case of consent to the receipt of electronic advertising, your consent can be revoked by clicking on the unsubscribe link. In such a case, processing will be stopped unless there is another legal basis to continue it. The legality of the data processed until the revocation shall not be affected by the revocation.

Other rights of data subjects

You also have the right to information, correction, deletion and restriction of the processing of personal data. If the legal basis for the processing of your personal data is your consent or in a contract concluded with you, you also have the right to data portability. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the supervisory authority. For more information on the supervisory authorities in the European Union, please click here.
