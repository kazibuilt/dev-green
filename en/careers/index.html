Whether research, development, marketing or production - at GRASS, teamwork is key when it comes to shaping the future of living. Because we know: Pioneering innovations and unique product solutions can only emerge in a special company culture.

Do you really want to get things moving in your job? Then you've come to the right place.

Each of our employees contributes to our excellent movement design. Our mission: Turning opening and closing furniture into an experience. We open up a variety of opportunities for our employees. So your creativity will know no limits.

Your benefits

That's what GRASS has to offer.

Moving forward together.

Movement is what drives us - this includes your professional advancement at GRASS. Benefit from a variety of internal career opportunities.

Modern and attractive working environment.

Exclusive GRASS/Würth employee discounts.

There are a number of discounts available to you as a GRASS employee - such as discounts on travel, in online shops and at the local gym.

Lunch subsidy.

Fancy a fresh and healthy lunch? Our Digibon system gives you discounts in a number of catering establishments.

Access to the Würth Academy.

Our employees' continuous professional development is important to us. In partnership with external educational institutes as well as with the Würth Academy, we offer numerous professional and personal further training opportunities.

Company doctor.

The health of our employees is close to our heart. That is why we place a great deal of importance on the promotion of health in the workplace.

Flexible working hours.

You can organise your work as you choose with our flex-time model. Flexibility is part of our culture of innovation.

Work from home option.

Varied and responsible tasks.

There are no limits to your creativity with us. At GRASS, we all get stuck in together and take responsibility.

Work travel subsidy.

You will receive a half-price travel ticket for the journey between your residence and place of work.
