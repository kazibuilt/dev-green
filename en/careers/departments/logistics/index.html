Logistics.

To offer a world-class logistics service - that is our aspiration. The GRASS logistics team keeps our production and sales network working day after day. Our employees are passionate about ensuring that everything arrives on time.

Logistics

So that everything lands where it belongs.

Our logistics experts work non-stop to ensure that the deadline requirements of our customers are met reliably. And, of course, as soon as possible.

But to know what happens where and when and why, you need talent as well as curiosity. This is precisely why we are always on the lookout for talented logistics specialists who, as team players, contribute their ideas, expertise and creative solutions to keeping GRASS in motion.
