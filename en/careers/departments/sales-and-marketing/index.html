Are you in sales and marketing? Do you like to work closely with customers? Do you want an employer that really gets things moving? Then you've come to the right place. We get people excited about GRASS. With commitment, joy and enthusiasm.

Sales and Marketing

We make technology a matter of emotion.

Sharing enthusiasm, arousing interest and generating demand in the global furniture market – that's what our sales and marketing employees are passionate about. This ensures not only our brand appeal, but also our long-term success. To maintain our leading position into the future, we are always looking for creative sales and marketing experts who set new standards when it comes to marketing our products throughout their entire lifecycle.

TEAM@SALES+MARKETING
