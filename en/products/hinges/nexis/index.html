Nexis

Click-on Technology.

The proven Click-on technology in the hinge arm forms a solid base for fastening the hinges to the cabinet without the need for any tools. Simply press with a finger and the hinge will lock into place on the mounting plate with an audible "click". The hinge can be detached from the Click-on mounting plate simply by unlocking it.

Soft-close

You like quiet? The Soft-close adapter can be retrofitted by simply clicking it on. Fast. Safe. Tool-free.
