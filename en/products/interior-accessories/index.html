The perfect interior accessories for your drawer.

SIMPLE. ATTRACTIVE. TIDY.

We need chaos. Because we love creating order.

TAVINEA offers carefully thought-out and lovingly designed interior accessories to organise your drawers. From high-quality metal structural elements to useful accessories made of wood - TAVINEA makes organisation a pleasure.

About TAVINEA

Knives, forks, spoons - we all use them at every meal. They are probably the utensils we use most in our lives. Giving these and many other everyday objects a beautiful, orderly home, making optimal use of existing storage space, ensuring fast access and simplifying workflows - that is the definition of TAVINEA by GRASS.
